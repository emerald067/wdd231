Final Project Site Plan

Pawprint Valley Animal Rescue

Site Name

Pawprint Valley Animal Rescue

Reason for Selection: This name clearly communicates the organization's purpose (animal rescue) while establishing a local, community-focused identity ("Valley"). It evokes imagery of animal tracks and a specific geographic area, making it memorable and approachable. The name is professional enough for a non-profit while remaining warm and inviting to potential adopters and donors.

Domain: pawprintvalleyrescue.org is chosen for the site.

Site Purpose

The Pawprint Valley Animal Rescue website serves as the primary digital hub for the organization's community outreach and operations. It provides:

  • Information about the rescue's mission, values, and operations for potential adopters and volunteers.
  • A regularly updated gallery of currently available animals for adoption with basic profiles.
  • Clear pathways for community involvement through volunteer applications and donation information.
  • Essential contact details and operational information for public inquiries.

Scenarios

These questions represent what target audience members might ask when visiting the site:

Potential Adopter

"I'm looking to adopt a cat that's good with children. What animals are currently available, and what does the adoption process involve?"

Community Volunteer

"I have weekend availability and would like to help with animal care. How can I apply to volunteer, and what types of roles are available?"

Local Business Owner

"Our company is interested in sponsoring a fundraising event. Who should we contact about partnership opportunities?"

Color Scheme

The color scheme is designed to be calming, professional, and accessible with sufficient contrast.

Primary Color

Headers, primary buttons, accent borders

Secondary Color

#F4F1DE (Cream)

Page backgrounds

Accent Color 1

#E07A5F (Terracotta)

Call-to-action buttons, highlights

Accent Color 2

#3D405B (Dark Blue)

Footer backgrounds, secondary text

Text Color

#1A1A1A (Near Black)

Primary body text

Typography

Headings: Montserrat

A clean, modern sans-serif font that conveys professionalism and approachability. Used for all page titles and section headers (h1-h3).

Body Text: Open Sans

A highly readable, web-optimized sans-serif font with excellent accessibility characteristics. Used for all paragraph content, form labels, and navigation items.

Special Cases: Comic Neue

A friendly, slightly playful font that adds warmth to pet names in the adoption gallery without compromising readability.
